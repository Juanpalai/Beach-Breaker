# Beach-Breaker

![Beach-Breaker Logo](https://github.com/Juanpalai/Porfolio/blob/main/images/BeachBreaker.png)

**About:**

Beach-Breaker is an engaging Match 3 game developed in Unity specifically designed for Android devices. Dive into a captivating gaming experience where strategic thinking meets limited moves. Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible by making every move count. The game introduces a unique mechanic allowing players to move tiles in any direction, adding an extra layer of complexity and creativity to the gameplay.

**Features:**

- **Match 3 Mechanics:** Classic matching gameplay with a twist, keeping players on their toes.
  
- **Limited Moves Challenge:** Strategize each move carefully within a limited number of movements to maximize your score.
  
- **Intuitive Controls:** Easy-to-use controls for seamless gameplay on Android devices.
  
- **Vibrant Graphics:** Enjoy a visually stunning experience with vibrant graphics and an immersive beach-themed design.
  
- **Innovative Tile Movement:** Break free from traditional constraints by moving tiles in any direction, requiring a fresh approach to puzzle-solving.

**How to Play:**

1. Download and install the game on your Android device [itch.io](https://tradocks.itch.io/beach-breaker).
2. Launch the app and start a new game.
3. Match at least three tiles of the same type to score points.
4. Plan your moves strategically within the limited number of movements.
5. Explore the innovative freedom of moving tiles in any direction for a unique gaming experience.
